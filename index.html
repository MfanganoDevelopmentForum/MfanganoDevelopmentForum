About Mfangano Development Forum

Mfangano Development Forum is a registered C.B.O championing the development Agenda of the People of Mfanagno ward as guided by 9 thematic areas icluding:~

  • Education & Vocational Training
  • Transport & Infastructure
  • Health & Sanitation
  • Planning, Finance & Entreprise Development
  • Land, Water & Environment
  • Agriculture, Livestock & Fisheries
  • Sports, Gender, Talent Development & Management
  • Language, Culture, Heritage & Tourism
  • Public Administration, Legal Affairs & Security

MDF was formally registered on 30th August 2021 with the Department of Social Development under the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection. It is registered as a Community Based Organization (CBO) with registration number, CBO.2106/21. 220 members as at 22nd December 2021.
